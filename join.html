Interested in Joining?

DSA is funded by our membership — not corporate foundations or private companies. This ensures that DSA members are in full democratic and political control of our organization. Through national dues and local contributions, DSA members self-fund our own tools for liberation.

If you are interested in learning more about GLDSA before joining our organization, fill out this interest form and one of our members will get back to you.

Becoming a DSA Member

Ready to join DSA? To become a DSA member you must pay dues to our national organization. Click here to become a member of the Democratic Socialists of America. Annual dues are an option, but we encourage everyone who is financially able to join as a monthly Movement Builder, since a greater proportion of those dues directly support GLDSA’s organizing work. If you are unable to pay dues to National you can fill out a Dues Waiver Form here.

After joining, you will receive a confirmation from the national DSA office. Forward your confirmation email to greater.lafayette.dsa@gmail.com so we can verify your membership. You will then be onboarded to our Slack, which is our chapter’s primary internal communications platform.
